1. Joining Formalities
1(a) New joiner has to fill join application form & Medical declaration
1(b) New Joiner has to submit their respective documents on the date of joining and fulfill their appointment letter with in 1 day without fail
1(c) No fake certificates and experience letters will be encouraged. If any one found it will be considered into legal action
1(d) Passport should be mandatory at the time of joining, if not with in 1 month they have to get it done
1(e) After Completion of Joining formalities, work space , Bio-metric & Logins will be provided
2. Office working Timings
2(a) 6 DAYS - 6 days, General, A and B Shift
2(b) General Shift - 9.00 am - 6.30 pm
2(c) First Shift - 6 .00 am - 3.00 pm
2(d) Second Shift - 3.00 pm – 12.00 am
2(e) Break Time - 10 min
2(f) Lunch or dinner - 45 min
2(g) Late coming - No grace period allowed. In failed case , consider half day loss of pay. Late working should not be reason for late coming.
3. Attendance - Leaves
3(a) Everyday Punching must be done by you for entry and exit
3(b) Everyday you should sign in register for entry and exit timings
3(c) Everyday you should mention entry and exit timings in the time sheets
3(d) You need to submit your Time Sheets every month on or before 20th else salary will not be processed
3(e) Late login will leads to LOP, make sure you are providing valid reason in MAIL or SMS that needs to be approved from your reporting manager. Verbal communication will be strictly avoided
3(f) Working on holiday and availing comp off should be approved from your reporting manager. Else It will be considered as working day
3(g) Leaves need to be intimated two weeks prior, sudden leave intimation will be considered as LOP
3(h) Medical emergency leave should be communicated to your reporting manager by MAIL or SMS. Medical certificate is mandatory to consider the leave as Sick leave
3(i) Continuous 3 Days absence to office without any information will leads to termination

4(a) Attire should be in Formal dress from Monday to Thursday
4(b) Casual dress is allowed on Friday and Saturday
4(c) Formal shoes is mandatory in week days, we don’t allow sandals or slippers to office
4(d) Fair hair cut and shaving should be maintained everyday

4(f) Hats & Caps will not be allowed
4(g) Wearing ID card in working timinings is mandatory. If you loose ID card, you need to inform immediately to HR Team. Permanent loss of ID will be penalized and you need to pay 500rs/- to get new ID card
